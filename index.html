About Us

Every Line Tells a Story

At Loco Ink Tattoo Studio, we believe tattoos are more than ink on skin — they are personal stories, memories, and expressions carved into a canvas of flesh. Nestled in the heart of Bali, we specialize in clean and timeless line art tattoos that speak louder than words.
Our mission is simple: to turn your vision into meaningful body art. Whether it’s your first tattoo or your tenth, we work with care, creativity, and a deep respect for the art form. Hygiene, precision, and custom design are the cornerstones of our craft.
Each piece we create is tailored to reflect your journey, your identity, your soul. Because here at Loco Ink, every line matters — and every tattoo is a legacy.
